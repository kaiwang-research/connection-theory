


# Connection Theory

> The official knowledge hub for Connection Theory.

**Understanding Persistent Adaptive Systems**

Connection Theory is a long-term research project exploring the fundamental principles that govern persistent adaptive systems across intelligence, biology, artificial intelligence, society, and civilization.


---

## Mission

To develop a unified framework for understanding organization, adaptation, intelligence, and long-term persistence across natural and artificial systems.

---

## Vision

Connection Theory aims to become an evolving knowledge system rather than a single publication.

The project is designed to grow continuously through research papers, technical notes, essays, experiments, and living documentation.

## Project Philosophy

Connection Theory is developed as a living knowledge system.

Concepts are defined once and continuously refined.

Papers, websites, and books are different representations of the same underlying knowledge base.

## What is Connection Theory?

Connection Theory investigates whether diverse adaptive systems can be understood through common organizational principles.
