# About Connection Theory

Connection Theory is an independent long-term research project exploring the fundamental principles that govern persistent adaptive systems.

## Mission

Our mission is to develop a unified conceptual framework capable of describing adaptive systems across multiple disciplines.

## Research Scope

Current research includes:

- Foundations of Connection Theory
- Constraint Generation Theory (CGT)
- Flow Shaping Principle (FSP)
- Evolutionary Relativity
- Artificial Intelligence
- Collective Intelligence

## Current Status

Connection Theory is currently under active development.
